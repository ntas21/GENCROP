# Contributing to GENCROP

Thank you for your interest in contributing to GENCROP.

We welcome developers, researchers, agricultural experts and open-science contributors interested in decentralized agricultural infrastructure.

---

## Contribution Areas

- Mobile-edge AI
- Agricultural datasets
- Soil intelligence systems
- Seed Passport infrastructure
- Solana integration
- Documentation improvements
- Open scientific collaboration

---

## Development Philosophy

GENCROP is built around:
- Open science
- Decentralized collaboration
- Biodiversity preservation
- Farmer participation
- Transparent infrastructure

---

## Future Collaboration

Public contribution workflows and development guidelines will expand as the protocol infrastructure evolves.
