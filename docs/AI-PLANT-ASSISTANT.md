# GENCROP AI Plant Assistant

## Overview

GENCROP AI Plant Assistant is designed to help farmers, gardeners and home growers monitor plant health using AI-powered agricultural intelligence.

The system analyzes:

- Soil conditions
- Humidity
- Temperature
- Plant symptoms
- Irrigation patterns
- Environmental conditions

---

## Core Features

### Plant Health Score
AI-generated health scoring for plants and crops.

### Fertilizer Recommendation
Suggests nutrient adjustments based on environmental conditions.

### Disease Detection
Detects possible fungal infections, nutrient deficiencies and environmental stress.

### Climate Monitoring
Analyzes local weather conditions and irrigation risks.

### Smart Irrigation
Provides watering recommendations using humidity and temperature data.
